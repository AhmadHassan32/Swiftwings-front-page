PROJECTS



Glide Drone

Glide drones are unmanned aerial vehicles designed for air dropping essential supplies to remote or hard-to-reach areas. They are equipped with gliding capabilities, allowing them to navigate accurately to designated drop zones and release payloads such as medical supplies, food, or humanitarian aid packages.

Cinematic Drones

Cinematic drones are specialized unmanned aerial vehicles equipped with high-resolution cameras and stabilized gimbal systems. They are used in the film and entertainment industry to capture smooth and dynamic aerial footage for movies, TV shows, commercials, and other video productions.

Reconnaissance Drone

Reconnaissance drones are unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) equipped with sensors and cameras used for gathering intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) purposes. They provide real-time aerial imagery and data to military, law enforcement, and other security agencies for situational awareness and decision-making.
